It's not nessecary to code something from scratch that many other people have done, like button simulators. On Roblox, there are many incremental games which match this specific game mechanism, pressing a simple button, get currency, trade in all your currency for another currency, and that goes on.

Introducing HBS Kit! A efficient, lag-reduced button simulator kit that constantly maintains updates and supports large numbers. This kit is user-friendly, has readable code, and is flexible in customization. You can tune this kit as much as you want to your liking, but make sure to also not break it.

NOTE: This webpage is not finished and may not contain all the information to tune the kit.

Why use HBS kit?

If you care about performance, flexibility, and readability, you should definitely use this kit! Here is the comparison table between HBS and EDBS (Exponential's Difficulty Button Simulator Kit). Do you want to achieve bigger numbers with efficiency? HBS uses AlyaNum, a fork of OmegaNum that is designed to be way faster. Because of this, players will gain smoother gameplay and less lag.
